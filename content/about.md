# About Obli-SSG

Obli-SSG is part of the poly-ssg ecosystem, demonstrating static site generation in Oberon.

## Why Oberon?

Oberon offers a minimalist, strongly-typed approach to systems programming. Its clean syntax and module system make it ideal for building reliable, maintainable tools. Obli-SSG showcases how Oberon's discipline translates to predictable, efficient site generation.
